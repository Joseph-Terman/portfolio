Thesis

Mobile and IoT Security

My thesis involved the research and discussion of threats and vulnerabilities related to the communication between a home security IoT device and its iOS mobile app.

I had ultimately discovered several ways to interrupt communication between the device and the app thus rendering the device useless. Ultimately, I was able to build a simple mobile app that allowed the user to monitor the IoT device and determine when the device had gone offline.
